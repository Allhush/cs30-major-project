# Major project proposal

## Description
- Wave defense game, you recruit troops to combat hordes of enemies that come at you in waves becoming progressively more difficult.

## Needs to have
- Money collected from enemies to buy troops
- several troop types with different attacks
  - sword troop
  - archer
  - healer
  - mage
- several enemy types with different attacks
  - zombie
  - skeleton
  - buff zombie
  - healer/archer targeting zombie
- Waves that become more difficult over time
  - more monsters
  - monsters have more health
  - increased monster variation
- at least 5 waves
- at least 1 boss monster at wave 5
- ability to move troops to desired areas
  - select troops and move them to a certain area
- sound effects
   - sword slash
   - bow release
   - healing aura
   - magic ray?
- powerups
  - ability to heal troops
  - boost troops attack
  - etc.

## Nice to have
- 10+ waves
- endless mode
- multiple boss monsters
- cool music
- sandbox mode
  - infinite money
  - ability to place enemies
  - etc.
- difficulty levels
- Upgrade existing troops
